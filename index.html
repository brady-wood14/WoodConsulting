Wood Consulting

Project Description:

Over the summer of 2025 I am aiming to launch a FREE personal consulting initiative aimed at supporting small businesses in the Minneapolis area. The goal is to apply structured problem solving, customer insight, and operational analysis to help local business owners identify actionable strategies for growth.

Services

  • Identify paths to increase revenue, expand market reach, or improve customer retention.
  • Analyze workflows to uncover bottlenecks in processes and find cost-saving opportunities.
  • Assess online footprint and provide informed strategies to boost engagement and visibility.

What to Expect

Step 1: Initital Engagement

We'll schedule a 1 hour call or in person meeting to learn more about your business, your goals, and the challenges you're facing.

↓
Step 2: Diagnostics

I’ll take a closer look at your operations, marketing, or customer experience — depending on your needs. This may include observing workflows, reviewing your digital presence, or gathering light customer feedback.

↓
Step 3: Strategy Development

Within 1-2 weeks, I’ll send you a short, actionable strategy brief with 2–3 tailored recommendations. Each suggestion includes data backed observations, rationale, and next steps.

↓
Step 4: Follow-Up Support

We’ll schedule a quick follow-up to go over the strategy and answer any questions. I’ll remain available to support implementation or connect you with helpful resources — all at no cost.
